What is DecaFans, and how to contribute?

DecaFans is a website for people who are interested in the DecaGear headset.
The community made it to help share the latest information leaked and shared on the DecaGear Discord server.
You can contribute to the site by sharing any information you have related to the DecaGear headset by going to the Leaks tab.

Feel free to also contribute to the development of the site on Github. Bugs reports and suggestions are also greatly appreciated.

What is there on this Site?

In the Leaks tab, you will find the latest information about the DecaGear headset gathered and shared by the community.
In Official news, you can find the Official channels for Megadodo. Most of this info is also in the discord.
